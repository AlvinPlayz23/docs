# Introduction to PROJECT-X

PROJECT-X is an AI-powered website builder that helps you create professional websites quickly using natural language prompts. Our platform leverages cutting-edge AI technologies to:

- Generate complete website structures from simple descriptions
- Provide intelligent content suggestions based on your needs
- Automate repetitive design and development tasks

## AI Providers

PROJECT-X integrates with leading AI providers including:

- OpenAI (GPT models for content generation)
- Stability AI (Stable Diffusion for image generation)
- Anthropic (Claude for advanced reasoning)

## Prompt Documentation

To get the best results from PROJECT-X's AI features:

1. Be specific about your website's purpose and audience
2. Provide clear examples of desired style and tone
3. Break complex requests into smaller, focused prompts
4. Use our built-in prompt templates for common website types
